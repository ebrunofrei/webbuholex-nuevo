# Linguistic Governance Policy — Intérprete Jurídico

## Scope
This policy governs the Multilingual Legal Interpreter tool.

## Data Handling
- No audio recordings are stored.
- No transcribed or translated text is stored.
- No content is logged.

## Telemetry
- Aggregated metrics only (counts, duration, mode).
- No personal data beyond anonymized identifiers.

## Consent
- Use of the tool constitutes implicit consent for real-time processing.
- No secondary use of data.

## Purpose Limitation
- Sole purpose: linguistic interpretation for access to justice.

## Retention
- Metrics retained for operational analysis only (TTL enforced).

## Auditability
- The system is auditable to demonstrate non-retention of content.
